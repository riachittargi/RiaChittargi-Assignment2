Chicken Tacos Recipe

List of ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tortillas
  • Mexican Cheese
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Chopped Onions
    • Seasonings
    • Garlic Powder
    • Onion Powder
    • Paprika
    • Adobo Seasoning
    • Salt
    • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Clean and wash your chicken
  2. Add some oil to a pan
  3. Throw your chicken in and add 1 tsp of each seasoning
  4. Add 1 tsp of black pepper and salt
  5. Cook your chicken on each side for 5 minutes
  6. Add lime and add the chicken into the tortillas
  7. Add pieces of cilantro and onions in tacos

Equipment

  1. Spatula
  2. Plates
  3. Pan
    • Heat up some oil in the pan

How to serve

  1. Top with more onions,cilatros and lime
  2. Add salsa to your liking and enjoy!
